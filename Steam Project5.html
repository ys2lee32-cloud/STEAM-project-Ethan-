What [CV]s will be controlled? How? Why?
same drop height of balls 1cm above the ramp use a ruler to measure same dropping height/distance=10cm above the ramp Because if we drop the balls all at different heights, the time will be affected. When we drop it lower, the time will decrease as it has a shorter path to travel. Time will decrease even if they get a bit less potential energy by falling lower. On the other hand when it is dropped higher, time will increase.
same type,structure of machine/ramp Same wooden ramp made with 60 degrees length 70cm do not change or rebuild the ramp during the experiment If the ramp’s angle has changed or surface has changed or length of it has changed during the experiment, it will affect the time (which we measure). When the angle gets larger , the time will increase. Same as length, if it gets longer, the time will also increase. Also if the surface of the ramp changes by cardboard to other elements during the experiment, it will also affect time and friction.
same environment same environment with no wind or distraction do not change or rebuild the ramp during the experiment If we were doing it somewhere it is windy and do the rest in somewhere very calm without any distraction, the measurement will be inaccurate because wind can change direction of the ball or push the ball so it gets more force.
same size of the ball same 2cm Do not change to other ball which has different size If the size changes, like if one has larger size, that larger ball will get more air resistance.
same force given when it gets dropped do not push as we drop. just release the ball. Do not push as we drop. Just release it without giving strength in my hand If I give a little push the speed and time will unfortunately be affected where those increases.
same mass of the ball about 500~1000g. use weight scaler to measure the weight of the ball If the weight is heavier it will roll faster and decrease the time and result in inaccurate measurement.
